# Code of Conduct

## Preamble

InsightOS is committed to providing a welcoming, inclusive, and harassment-free experience for all community members, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, political belief, race, religion, sex, sexual identity and orientation, socioeconomic status, or technical ability.

This code of conduct outlines our expectations for participant behavior in the InsightOS community and the consequences for unacceptable behavior.

## Our Commitment

We are committed to:
- Creating a safe and respectful environment
- Valuing diverse perspectives and experiences
- Supporting each other's growth and learning
- Maintaining professional and collaborative communication
- Addressing conflicts constructively

## Expected Behavior

All community members are expected to:

### Be Respectful
- Treat all people with dignity and respect
- Value differences of opinion and experience
- Listen actively and seek to understand others' perspectives
- Assume good faith in others' contributions

### Be Inclusive
- Welcome newcomers and help them feel valued
- Create an environment where everyone can participate
- Use inclusive language and avoid assumptions
- Actively work to make discussions accessible

### Be Professional
- Provide constructive feedback
- Focus on ideas rather than personal attacks
- Acknowledge and correct mistakes gracefully
- Maintain confidentiality when appropriate

### Be Collaborative
- Work together toward common goals
- Share knowledge and help others learn
- Celebrate each other's successes
- Support the broader community

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are considered unacceptable and will not be tolerated:

### Harassment
- Offensive comments related to age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender, nationality, personal appearance, political belief, race, religion, sex, sexual orientation, socioeconomic status
- Sexual harassment or unwelcome sexual attention
- Deliberate intimidation, stalking, or following
- Sustained disruption of community events or conversations
- Non-consensual photography or recording

### Abuse and Threats
- Threats of violence or violent language
- Personal attacks or insults
- Sustained, deliberate harassment
- Doxxing (sharing private information)
- Encouraging others to harass or abuse

### Discrimination
- Exclusion based on protected characteristics
- Discriminatory language or imagery
- Hostile behavior toward particular groups
- Systemic bias or prejudice

### Professional Misconduct
- Plagiarism or intellectual property theft
- Spam or commercial promotion without consent
- Spreading misinformation
- Attempting to manipulate voting or discussions
- Abusing reporting mechanisms

## Reporting and Enforcement

### Reporting Violations

If you witness or experience unacceptable behavior, please report it:

**Email**: conduct@insightos.dev

**Include in your report:**
- Description of the incident
- Participants involved
- When and where it occurred
- Any context you have
- Your contact information (optional)

All reports will be treated confidentially. We will not disclose the identities of reporters without consent unless necessary for investigation.

### Investigation Process

1. **Receipt**: We acknowledge receipt within 48 hours
2. **Investigation**: We conduct a fair, thorough investigation
3. **Response**: We take appropriate action
4. **Follow-up**: We follow up with relevant parties

### Consequences

Depending on the severity and nature of violations, consequences may include:

- Private warning or conversation
- Public apology requirement
- Temporary suspension from community
- Permanent removal from community
- Report to appropriate authorities (if illegal)

## Appeals

If you believe a decision was made in error, you may appeal to conduct-appeals@insightos.dev within 7 days of notification. Your appeal will be reviewed by a different person or committee.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to:
- GitHub repositories and discussions
- Community forums and chat channels
- Conferences and events organized by InsightOS
- Official social media accounts
- Any other community spaces

This code applies equally to all community members, including maintainers and leadership.

## Enforcement

Our goal is to foster a positive, inclusive community. Enforcement is applied consistently and fairly. Repeated violations may result in escalating consequences.

## Acknowledgments

This code of conduct is adapted from the Contributor Covenant and practices from other successful open-source communities.

## Questions?

For questions about this code of conduct, please email conduct@insightos.dev.
